My Record Player!

Here is my record player!

It is a Jensen JTA-220 that I got for roughly $8.00 at the Goodwill. Some record experts might say its terrible, but I say its great for the price.

Record players work on a very simple principle. There is a little groove etched with bumps analogous to sound. When a needle is placed in the groove as the disc rotates, the vibrations are made into an electrical signal that is amplified into audible music.
